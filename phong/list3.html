RECRUITMENT SPECIALIST

Marketing Division - At Office Star Class Ho Chi Minh - Full Time

Job description:

  • Managing the Brand and Advertising department with the following areas of expertise: Market research (research), brand management and communication (branding), making and implementing advertising/brand sponsorship plans (media) , develop creative ideas and organize the production of brand communication products (creative);

  • Plan and budget for brand communication on a monthly/quarterly/yearly basis based on business goals, available resources, and market/category/customer insights, and organize the implementation of this plan;

  • Control and evaluate the results of the brand communication plans/activities and adjust/optimize these plans/activities in line with market changes and the Company's overall business goals;

  • Control marketing budget, ensure maximum effective spending within approved budget.

  • Collect, analyze and report information on the market: industry, competitors, customers, public policy in online education and advise leaders on opportunities and risks when investing into business activities;

  • Team building and development;

  • Building, amending and supplementing processes/regulations/forms about the functions of the department in charge;

  • Perform other tasks assigned by the Marketing Director and the Board of Directors.

Candidate Requirements:

  • Having extensive and practical knowledge of marketing activities (Preferably in the field of digital content);

  • Having the ability to organize, lead and operate in a methodical manner, the operating system of the marketing department;

  • Having skills in synthesizing, analyzing and evaluating market-related data to make judgments about opportunities and threats;

  • Skilled in researching and organizing market research;

  • Having knowledge and experience in organizing and implementing marketing communication programs with multi-tools, multi-channels, setting up and controlling marketing budgets;

  • Minimum 3 years of experience in a similar position.

  • Received advanced training in professional knowledge and skills.

  • Review and consider salary increase 2 times/year.

  • Young, open environment, ready to absorb new things, and focus on developing talents.
