Event Marketing Specialist

Job description:

  • Proposing ideas and scripts for company's online and offline events

  • Budgeting, checklists, searching and surveying locations, preparing necessary documents, planning and implementing events.

  • Connect and coordinate with other departments to ensure the event goes as planned.

  • Search and negotiate with partners and suppliers for the event.

  • Supervising production, coordinating events taking place as planned.

  • Complete procedures, valid documents with state agencies and suppliers to serve the event.

  • Evaluation and reporting after the event.

  • Perform other duties as assigned by superiors.

Candidate Requirements:

  • Minimum 1 year experience in similar position. Preference will be given to candidates with experience in the Education industry or agencies.

  • Basic knowledge of Production and Event Organization.

  • Have good skills in event planning and presentation.

  • Good thinking about time management, project management.

  • Attention to small details and deadlines.

  • Good office computer skills: Word, Excel, especially Power Point.

Interest:

  • 13th month salary and other benefits according to company regulations: birthday, vacation, vacation, holidays, social insurance, health insurance...

  • 12 days leave/year in accordance with labor law.

  • Enjoy the reward regimes of the leaders of the Company and the Group.

  • Participate in training courses to improve professional skills.

  • Dynamic, friendly working environment, with many opportunities for training, learning and development.
