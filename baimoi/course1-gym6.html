Swimming Course Details

The Swimming Fundamentals course is designed for beginners who want to learn how to swim or improve their basic swimming skills. This course covers the essential techniques and water safety practices necessary for swimming proficiency. Participants will learn proper body positioning, breathing techniques, basic strokes, and water safety skills, enabling them to feel confident and comfortable in the water.

Course Objectives:

>>Learn Water Safety Skills: Understand essential water safety practices, including entering and exiting the water safely, floating, treading water, and basic rescue techniques.

>>Develop Proper Body Positioning: Learn correct body positioning and balance in the water to improve efficiency and reduce drag.

>>Master Breathing Techniques: Understand and practice proper breathing techniques while swimming, including rhythmic breathing and breath control.

>>Learn Basic Swimming Strokes: Acquire the fundamentals of the front crawl (freestyle) and backstroke, including arm movements, leg kicks, and coordination.

>>Gain Water Confidence and Comfort: Build confidence and comfort in the water through various exercises and drills.

>>Improve Coordination and Propulsion: Enhance coordination between arm and leg movements for efficient propulsion through the water.

>>Understand Pool Etiquette and Lap Swimming: Familiarize yourself with pool etiquette, lane swimming rules, and basic lap swimming techniques.

What you'll learn?

>>Water safety practices and basic rescue techniques.

>>Proper body positioning and balance in the water.

>>Breathing techniques for swimming.

>>Basic front crawl (freestyle) and backstroke.

>>Water confidence and comfort-building exercises.

>>Coordination of arm and leg movements for efficient swimming.

>>Pool etiquette and lap swimming techniques.

Requirements

>>No prior swimming experience is necessary.

>>Comfortable swimwear and goggles.

>>Access to a swimming pool or suitable aquatic facility.

>>Personal flotation devices (if required or recommended by the instructor).
